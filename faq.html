The Jacks' Hotel - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is until 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request.

2. Do you offer free Wi-Fi?

Yes, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available in all rooms and public areas.

3. Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we have free on-site parking for all guests.

4. Do you allow pets?

We are a pet-friendly hotel, but prior notification is required. Additional charges may apply.

5. How can I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation online via our website, call us directly, or email our booking team.

6. What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations up to 24 hours before the check-in date are free. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur charges.
