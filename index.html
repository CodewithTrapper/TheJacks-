ABOUT
THE JACKS' HOTEL

Jacks’ Hotel is a medium sized hospitality establishment that provides accommodation, dining and recreational services to both local and international guests. The hotel aims to offer quality and comfortable services at affordable prices while maintaining high standards of customer satisfaction. The hotel has 60 employees divided across several departments such as Front Office Department (Receptionist, Reservation Clerk & Accounts), Food & Beverage Department and ICT Department (IT Support and Maintenance). The hotel has 50 rooms, a restaurant, conference halls and a swimming pool, making it mid-level hospitality organization and operates 24/7. The Jacks’ Hotel runs several operations which includes Room Booking and reservation where guests can book rooms in advance or upon arrival, customer check-in and check-out done by the receptionist through registering guests, assigning rooms and billing upon departures. In the hotel the meals and drinks are also prepared and served by Food and Beverage department led by the Executive chef for food preparation and cooking while the waiters serve meals and drinks to guests.
